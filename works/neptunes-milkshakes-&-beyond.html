Neptune's Milkshakes & Beyond

Branding • Digital Marketing



A school project for the Brand Management course in iAcademy in the Philippines. The task was to rebrand an existing milkshake shop, named Neptune's Milkshakes & Beyond and make mock up posters, while still following the brand's theme. Project preview includes mock up introductory, institutional, teaser and seasonal posters.
