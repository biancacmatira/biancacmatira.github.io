Earth Hour Interactive Poster

Print Design



A school project for the Print Communication course in iAcademy in the Philippines. The task was to make an event poster using Silkscreen Method. The bonus interactive feature, glow-in-the-dark paint, matched the chosen event, Earth Hour 2014. Project preview includes expected before (lights on) and after (lights off) look of the poster, and the actual before and after photos.
